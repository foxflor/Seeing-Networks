Wellington City Manhole Covers

Manholes are underground spaces to access systems such as water pipes, sewers, electric wires and telephone connections.

Manhole Cover 1 - GAS

Manhole covers are used to show what the manhole contains. This is a gas manhole. The colour yellow is used as this is considered a more dangerous manhole.

Manhole Cover 2 - WCC

Due to the large increase in technology, connections have been moved underground. This removes all the wires and connections from sight and it protects these wires from damage.
